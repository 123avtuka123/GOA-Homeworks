• French toast
    1. Grab a bowl.
    2. Break an egg.
    3. Put the yolk inside.
    4. Scramble it.
    5. Put salt and milk in the bowl.
    6. Heat a pan.
    7. Put oil on it.
    8. Put bread in the egg and soak it in.
    9. Put the bread in the pan.
    10. Flip it when the first side is toased.
    11. Put it on a plate when the other side is toasted.
    12. Enjoy!!!
